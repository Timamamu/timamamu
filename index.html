About me

I’m a Product Engineer trained in design engineering, focused on building end-to-end systems that move from concept to deployment.

I hold a Master’s in Design Engineering from Harvard University, with additional training in Computer Vision at University College London and Electrical Engineering at Brunel University. My work integrates software, hardware, and intelligent functionality with a strong emphasis on usability and real-world performance.

I design system architectures, build core functionality, and refine features through iteration. My projects have served aviation, healthcare, consumer services, and operational environments, each with distinct constraints and performance requirements.

I care deeply about how people experience the systems I build, and I make technical decisions with clarity, usability, and reliability in mind.
